Psychotherapy

Clinical Practice

I am a licensed clinical psychologist practicing part-time at the UPMC Western Psychological Associates. I have expertise in several therapeutic modalities, including cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT).

I specialize in working with diagnostically diverse adults striving to reconnect with their core values and pursue a more meaningful, fulfilling life. My therapeutic approach incorporates mindfulness, behavioral change strategies, and experiential exercises to foster psychological flexibility, allowing clients to clarify their values and translate them into actionable steps that lead to a richer, more authentic life experience.

Therapeutic Approach

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Evidence-based approach focusing on identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns and behaviors.

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Helping clients accept difficult thoughts and feelings while committing to actions aligned with their values.

Mindfulness-Based Approaches

Incorporating present-moment awareness and non-judgmental observation to enhance psychological well-being.

Values-Based Living

Helping clients clarify their core values and take committed action toward a more meaningful life.

Clinical Supervision

I provide clinical supervision through the UPMC Western Psychiatric Hospital Clinical Psychology Internship Program.
